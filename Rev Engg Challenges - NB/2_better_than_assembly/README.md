# 🧩 Challenge Name: Are You Better Than Assembly?

**Category:** Reverse Engineering (ASCII Exploitation)  
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Points:** 500  

---

## 📜 Description

You’ve been given a mysterious file.

No source code.  
No binary.  
Just something that claims to be **“Better Than Assembly.”**

It greets you with confidence:

> *Only the chosen one will know what the flag is!*

Are you the chosen one?

Somewhere inside this strange low-level construct lies the real flag. But beware — not everything it prints is the truth. There may be decoys. There may be traps. There may be… fake victories.

---

## 🎯 Your Mission

- Understand what this file really is.  
- Figure out how it validates input.  
- Defeat its logic.  
- Recover the **real** flag.  

No brute force will save you.  
No guesswork will carry you through.  
Only careful analysis and a sharp understanding of bitwise logic will lead you to success.

Think deeper than assembly.  
Think structurally.  
Think like the compiler.

---

```markdown

DAKSHH{...}

```

**Good luck. The machine already doubts you.**
